## Project Overview

This repository is my **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, submitted as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is structured around five modules, each mapped to a specific task that demonstrates digital awareness, professional online presence, platform literacy, communication skills, and cybersecurity awareness.

I have taken on the role of a **Student Digital Ambassador** — helping peers navigate the digital world safely and professionally.

## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic (Module 1)
Created a one-page visual infographic using **Canva** covering: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The infographic is designed for first-year students who are new to the digital academic environment.
📁 See: `task-1-presentation/`

---

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. The GitHub profile README introduces my branch, year, and learning goals. LinkedIn displays current education details. Kaggle is set up for future data science exploration.
📁 See: `task-2-portfolio/`

---

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)
Completed a beginner challenge on **HackerRank** (Solve Me First). Also created a 5-question **Google Form** — "Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz" — that can be used to quiz batchmates.

🔗 **Google Form Quiz Link:** [https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdEBXi1gp6RqBr_517Ac9zbDabrLdIbqaSxaQSvIK2XWWcfyw/viewform?usp=publish-editor]

📁 See: `task-3-platforms/`

---

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide (Module 4)
Drafted two professional emails: one requesting an assignment extension from a professor, and one expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for college students.
📁 See: `task-4-email-etiquette/`

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness (Module 5)
Researched **UPI/Online Payment Fraud** — one of the most common cybercrimes targeting Indian college students. Wrote a 200-word case study and a practical prevention checklist with 8+ actionable tips, including UPI-specific safety measures and reporting channels.
📁 See: `task-5-cybercrime/`
